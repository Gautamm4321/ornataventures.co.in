⭐ ABOUT US

Ornata Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is a professionally managed Merchant Exporter & Importer from India, supplying Granite, Marble, Natural Stones, Engineered Stones, Raw Blocks, Slabs, and Cut-to-Size Tiles to global markets.

Our journey began nearly 10 years ago in Bangkok, Thailand, where our Director Mr. Biren Shah and his wife built a successful stone business from absolutely nothing — no capital, no background, no external support.

Through dedication and consistency, we became one of Thailand's leading importers of Indian Granite & Marble, supplying stockyards, contractors, fabricators, and developers across the country.

Today, we operate a 21,000 sq.ft. fully equipped stockyard in Thailand and a strong India-based export company managing everything from sourcing to container loading with complete professionalism.

Our India Network Includes:

  • Direct quarry connections
  • Region-specialized factories
  • Premium processing unit
  • Job work facilities for project supply
